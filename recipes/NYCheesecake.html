New York Cheesecake

Description

New York Cheesecake, with sour cream is foolproof, easy, and super delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 3(8 ounce) packages cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat - preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make the crust - Mix together graham crumbs, melted butter, and sugar in a medium bowl until evenly moistened. Press into the bottom and 1/2 inch up the sides of a 9-inch springform pan. Set aside.
  3. Make the cheesecake - Mix together cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth. Blend in eggs, vanilla, and cream of tartar. Pour over crust in the pan.
  4. Bake the cake - Bake in the preheated oven until the center is set, about 50 minutes. Allow to cool on the counter for 5 minutes.
  5. Make the topping - While cheesecake is cooling, make topping: Mix together sour cream, sugar, and vanilla in a medium bowl until smooth.
  6. Finish the cake - Spread sour cream mixture evenly over the top of the cheesecake. Return to oven for 5 minutes. Allow to cool on the counter for 5 minutes.
