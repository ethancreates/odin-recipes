Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a staple in many households. Lasagna, or "Lasagne" is a wide, flat pasta noodle, usually baked in layers in the oven.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2lbs Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat - Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles - Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Make the cheese layer - Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna - Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Then layer 1/3 of the noodles, 1/3 of the cheese mixture, and 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers two more times.
  5. Bake the lasagna - Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
